Our Journey

Founded with a simple yet powerful idea, Infinite Comfort started as a small manufacturing unit near Doon Valley College in Karnal, Haryana. What began as a passion for creating superior mattresses has grown into a thriving enterprise serving thousands of happy customers across northern India.

Our commitment to quality, affordability, and sustainability drives every decision we make. We use only the finest materials, employ skilled craftsmen, and follow rigorous quality control processes to ensure every Restico mattress meets our exacting standards.
